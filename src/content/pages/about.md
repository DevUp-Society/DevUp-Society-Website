## Our Mission

DEVTHON was founded with a singular mission: to democratize access to world-class technical education and innovation resources for students across India. We believe that the next breakthrough in technology—whether in Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, or SpaceTech—will come from a college dorm room. 

Our goal is to provide the platform, the mentorship, and the capital to turn those dorm room ideas into reality.

## The Problem We Solve

In many engineering colleges across India, there is a massive gap between academic curriculum and industry requirements. Students learn theory, but lack the practical, hands-on experience of building real software in a high-pressure, collaborative environment.

DEVTHON bridges this gap. By bringing together students, industry mentors, and corporate recruiters for an intensive 36-hour sprint, we simulate the environment of a fast-paced startup.

## What Makes DEVTHON Different?

1. **Product First:** We do not evaluate based on code elegance alone. We judge based on the product. Does it solve a real problem? Is there a viable business model? Is the user experience intuitive?
2. **Post-Event Incubation:** For most hackathons, the journey ends at the closing ceremony. At DEVTHON, the winning teams are funneled into the **DevUp Incubation Program**, receiving continued mentorship and seed capital to launch their products as real companies.
3. **Open Access:** We maintain a zero-fee registration policy. Innovation should be based on merit, not financial capability.

## The Team

DEVTHON is powered by the **DevUp Ecosystem**, a student-led organization based out of Hyderabad, Telangana. We are a collective of developers, designers, and community builders passionate about tech.

Join us in building the future.
