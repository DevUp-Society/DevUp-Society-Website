## General Information

### What is DEVTHON?
DEVTHON is a national-level innovation hackathon organized by DevUp Ecosystem. It is designed to bring together student developers, designers, and entrepreneurs to solve real-world problems over a 36-hour sprint.

### Who can participate?
Any student currently enrolled in an engineering college, university, or equivalent educational institution in India can participate. We also have special tracks for working professionals and startups.

### Is there a registration fee?
No. Registration and participation in DEVTHON are entirely free. We believe that innovation should not have a financial barrier.

## Team & Registration

### How many members can be in a team?
Teams can consist of a minimum of 2 and a maximum of 4 members.

### Can I participate solo?
While we strongly encourage team participation for the best experience, solo participants are allowed in specific domains. However, you will be competing against full teams.

### What if I don't have a team?
We host a team-building mixer on our Discord server two weeks prior to the event. You can pitch your ideas and find teammates with complementary skills.

## The Event

### What are the main domains/tracks?
The primary domains include Artificial Intelligence (AI), Machine Learning (ML), Cybersecurity, Web3 & Blockchain, and Tech for Social Good. 

### Will accommodation be provided?
Yes, for offline finals, we provide sleeping arrangements, high-speed internet, and all meals for the duration of the 36-hour hackathon.

### What should I bring?
Bring your laptop, chargers, a valid college ID card, any necessary hardware (if participating in IoT tracks), and a sleeping bag if you prefer your own.

## Prizes & Opportunities

### What is the total prize pool?
The total prize pool exceeds ₹5,00,000, distributed across various tracks and special sponsor challenges.

### Are there internship or placement opportunities?
Yes! Our corporate sponsors use DEVTHON as a primary recruitment drive. Top performers frequently receive on-the-spot interview offers for internships and full-time software engineering roles.
